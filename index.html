ABOUT US

Three Generations of Coffee Passions

Our Heritage

From a small corner shop to a
community landmark

Founded in 1950 by Giovanni Rossi, our coffee house began as a modest neighborhood spot. Today, we still use his original roasting techniques while innovating with modern brewing methods. Every cup carries the warmth of our family's dedication to the craft of perfect coffee.

Our Promise

We commit to sustainable sourcing, artisanal craftsmanship, and creating a space where every customer feels like family. Our beans travel directly from ethical growers to our vintage roaster, ensuring quality you can taste.

✓ Direct trade with coffee farmers
✓ Small-batch roasted weekly
✓ Recipes perfected over 70 years
